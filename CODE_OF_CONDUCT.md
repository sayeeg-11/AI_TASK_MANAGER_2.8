Experienced members are encouraged to guide and support new contributors. Provide clear and patient explanations, welcome questions, and assume good intentions. A friendly community helps the project grow and thrive.

Harassment, discrimination, insults, personal attacks, and inappropriate behavior are strictly prohibited. Avoid posting spam, irrelevant content, or excessive self-promotion in any project channels.

### 4. Enforcement and Consequences

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Violations will be reviewed and may result in:

* A warning
* Temporary suspension from discussions or contributions
* Permanent removal from the project's repositories and communication channels

The severity of the action depends on the nature of the violation.
